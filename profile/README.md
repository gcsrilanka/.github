# GitHub Community Sri Lanka

GitHub Community Sri Lanka, formed by GitHub Campus Experts in the country, is dedicated to empowering students to thrive in the field of technology. Our community is a platform designed to unite students from various regions of the country, fostering collaboration and the collective building of knowledge. We are committed to providing a space where students can grow, learn, and contribute to the vibrant tech landscape in Sri Lanka
